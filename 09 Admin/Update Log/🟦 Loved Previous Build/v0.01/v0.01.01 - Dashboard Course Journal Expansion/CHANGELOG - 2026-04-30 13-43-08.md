## Revert Notes

To revert this stage manually:
1. Remove the new folders inside:
   - 10 Courses
   - 09 Admin/Journal
   - 09 Admin/Integrations
2. Restore the previous Swift app from any saved backup if available.
3. Keep App Data CSV files if you want to preserve logs.
